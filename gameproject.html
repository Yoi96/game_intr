GAME BACKGROUND

This is a maze game. It tells the story of a 10-year-old girl who failed to commit suicide and became a persistent vegetative state. In the game, we can see what happened to this girl at the beginning, also when the player wins, the player also can see the girl's story.....

GAME PROPS

# health Bar

There are no items that can add health bars in this game. If the player steps on a trap or an object that deducts blood, it will reduce the blood volume by 20% or 100%. Also, if the player's health is 0%, the player will die and return to the main menu page.

How to play the game?

It is a maze game, the player can find the coins and key in this game. Press the space bar to jump. Press "M" on the keyboard to pause the game. or exit the game. Press "P" on the keyboard to check the map. You can see where you are by jumping above. 20% of the damage will be taken when encountering a trap. (If your HP becomes 0 will be forced to jump to the Main Menu page) This is a maze game to find an exit. Victory conditions: Find the key or coins.
