Poster Design

SOFTEWARE: Adobe Photoshop
    This is a composite image made with Adobe Photoshop. This poster is about a woman holding a falling star with her eyes closed in the forest. The stars represent hope, and the girl's eyes are closed to represent that she wants to gain hope for nothing.
    This design is inspired by the Chinese saying "Pies from the sky", which means that pie falling in the sky originally refers to the free and delicious food similar to pie falling in the sky, which generally refers to the chance of being missed in natural life. Some material or wealthy things that can satisfy people's desires happen for no reason; it is also derogatory to those who sit back and enjoy it. Later, people also used the opposite, saying that no pie will fall from the sky.
I used this picture to be the first background picture because it has meteors. Meteors and star mean hope or wish.
I used this picture to be the second background picture.
I used this picture to be the last one, and then add the woman picture and made the star in Adobe Photoshop.
